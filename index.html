The Grand Living Room

Residential Project | Completed Fall 2024

Project Summary

This project involved a complete redesign of a spacious suburban home's living area. The goal was to create a cozy yet elegant space that was perfect for both family gatherings and quiet evenings. We opted for a neutral color palette with rich textures and natural materials to bring warmth and a sense of timeless luxury.

  • **Key Elements:** Custom-built fireplace, large-format oak flooring, bespoke shelving.
  • **Materials:** Limestone, wool, linen, and reclaimed wood.
